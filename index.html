K(NOT) A CONVERSATION v2

Communication through knots and AI systems

This project was created for transmediale / Vorspiel & CTM

The sculpture is embedded with questions and AI-generated answers that reflect on the concepts of artificial intelligence, creativity and labour. Use your smartphone camera and scan the QR code on the right to connect and communicate with it. To navigate in the scene use the left mouse button (Rotate), right mouse button (Pan) and mouse wheel (Zoom).

A collaboration between Ambika Joshi, Nayeli Vega Vargas, and Thomas Heidtmann
